Gravity

Made in unity for school. In Gravity the player is tasked with bending their gravitational pull to navigate the space station.

The user can walk on any surface that they choose. As a programmer I was part of the team making sure this worked.

Handling rotations in Unity was harder than we thought but, as these examples show, we made it work.

Pope Fight: The Game

Made in JavaScript for school. An educational game teaching the player about the harsh reality of the papal election during the height of the renaissance.

To show that history is not a straight line and much more chaotic than a lot of people believe, the user hears news that happened near the year the game is set.

When the player enters the Apostine Palace they can vote on who they wish to become the next pope. In 9 times out of 10, this is themselves.

Cabin

Made in Unreal for Ludum Dare 47, visit here. A mystery repeating itself and the player is trapped in the middle of it. Can you escape this hellscape?
All the rooms are loaded in when they are needed and the player is moved using portals. The main loop of the game is shown here as the player is stuck in the house.

I was part of the team creating assets for the different rooms.
